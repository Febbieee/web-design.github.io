About

Tops Cebu Lookout is a popular tourist destination in Cebu, Philippines, known for its stunning panoramic view Perched on the hills of Busay, approximately 600 meters above sea level, this lookout offers unobstructed vistas of Cebu City, Mactan Island, and the surrounding mountains.

Insider Tips

  • Tops Cebu Lookout is located in the Barangay Busay area, just a short drive from Cebu City. The tops lookout is situated on a hilltop, offering visitors a breathtaking view of the city and beyond. To reach Tops, you can either drive your private vehicle or take public transportation.
  • If you’re driving, you can easily find your way to Tops by following the road signs from JY Square Mall in Lahug. The road leading to Tops is well-paved but can be narrow and winding, so exercise caution while driving. Motorcycle taxis, known as habal-habal, are also available for hire if you prefer a more adventurous ride.
  • For those relying on public transportation, you can take a jeepney or taxi from Cebu City to JY Square Mall. From there, you can hire a motorcycle taxi or take another jeepney that goes directly to Tops. It’s always a good idea to confirm with the driver that they are going to Tops Cebu Lookout to ensure a hassle-free journey.
  • To fully appreciate the beauty of Tops Cebu Lookout, it’s recommended to visit during sunrise or sunset. Arriving early in the morning allows you to witness the city come alive as the sun rises, casting a soft glow over the landscape. Alternatively, visiting during sunset offers a different perspective, as the city lights begin to twinkle against the darkening sky.
  • If you prefer a quieter experience, try to avoid peak hours, especially on weekends or holidays when the lookout can get crowded. Weekdays are generally less busy, allowing you to enjoy the serene atmosphere and have more space to capture that perfect shot.
  • Tops Cebu Lookout provides several facilities and amenities to enhance visitors’ experience. The lookout area has viewing decks equipped with binoculars, allowing you to get a closer look at the cityscape. There are also picnic areas where you can enjoy a leisurely meal while taking in the stunning views.
  • Dress comfortably:
    As the lookout is situated at a higher altitude, it can get chilly, especially during the early morning or late evening. Bring a light jacket or sweater to keep yourself warm.
  • Bring your camera or smartphone:
    Tops Cebu Lookout offers picture-perfect views, so don’t forget to capture the moment. Whether you’re a professional photographer or just want to snap some memorable shots, you’ll want to have your camera or smartphone handy.
  • Visit during weekdays:
    If possible, plan your visit for a weekday to avoid the crowds. This will give you a more relaxed and peaceful experience.
